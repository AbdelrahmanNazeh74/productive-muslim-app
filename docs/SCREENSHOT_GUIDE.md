# Screenshot Guide

Required screenshot sizes and recommended screens for Play Store and App Store submissions.

---

## Required Sizes

### Google Play Store

| Slot | Size | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Phone | 1080 × 1920 px (or 1440 × 3120 px) | Required. Min 2, max 8. |
| 7" Tablet | 1200 × 1920 px | Optional, improves tablet discoverability |
| 10" Tablet | 1600 × 2560 px | Optional |

- Format: JPG or 24-bit PNG (no alpha)
- Orientation: portrait recommended (landscape also accepted)
- No device frames required (add them for a more polished look)

### Apple App Store

| Slot | Device | Size | Required? |
|---|---|---|---|
| 6.7" | iPhone 15 Pro Max / 16 Plus | 1290 × 2796 px | **Required** |
| 5.5" | iPhone 8 Plus | 1242 × 2208 px | Required (for older device pages) |
| 6.1" | iPhone 15 / 16 | 1179 × 2556 px | Optional (covered by 6.7") |
| iPad Pro 12.9" 6th gen | iPad Pro M2 | 2048 × 2732 px | Required if iPad support enabled |
| iPad Pro 11" | iPad Pro M2 | 1668 × 2388 px | Optional |

- Format: JPG or PNG
- Orientation: portrait required for iPhone; landscape optional for iPad
- Up to 10 screenshots per device slot

## 5 Recommended Screens

Capture these screens in order — they tell the app's story from onboarding to daily use.

### Screen 1 — Timeline Dashboard (main hook)
**What to show:** The home timeline with prayer countdown strip, DailyProgressRing, and 2–3 time blocks visible (Fajr, Golden Hour, Deep Work).

**Caption:**
> "Your entire day, built around your prayers."

**How to trigger:** Open the app on a day with a full schedule. Ensure at least one prayer has been prayed (green checkmark) and one is upcoming (countdown showing).

---

### Screen 2 — Habit Streaks
**What to show:** The Habits page with several habits showing flame icons and streak counts (ideally 7+ day streaks), plus the Weekly Score card.

**Caption:**
> "Build streaks that never break unfairly."

**How to trigger:** Mark several habits complete across multiple days in development mode, then screenshot the Habits tab.

---

### Screen 3 — Ramadan Dashboard
**What to show:** The Ramadan tab with the night-sky background, live Iftar countdown, Suhoor/Iftar times, and the Tarawih block visible.

**Caption:**
> "Ramadan mode — automatically activated every year."

**How to trigger:** Enable Ramadan mode in Settings → Ramadan (toggle). Set device time to an afternoon hour so the Iftar countdown shows a meaningful value.

---

### Screen 4 — Analytics Dashboard
**What to show:** The Analytics tab open on the Heatmap or Prayer tab, showing at least 3 weeks of data with green/amber/red patterns on the heatmap calendar.

**Caption:**
> "See your consistency at a glance."

**How to trigger:** Use the app for several days or seed demo data, then screenshot the Heatmap view under Analytics.

---

### Screen 5 — Onboarding Step 3 — Prayer Settings
**What to show:** Onboarding Step 3 with the prayer calculation method selector visible (MWL, ISNA, etc.) and the Madhab toggle.

**Caption:**
> "Personalised for your location and madhab."

**How to trigger:** Clear app data, open the app fresh, step through onboarding to Step 3.

---

## Device frame recommendation

Add device frames using [Previewed](https://previewed.app) or [DaVinci Apps](https://www.davinciapps.com) (free web tools). Use:
- iPhone 15 Pro frame (black) for iOS screenshots
- Pixel 8 Pro frame (black) for Android screenshots

Keep frame style consistent across all 5 screenshots.

---

## Suggested caption layout (for framed screenshots)

Use a two-zone layout:
```
┌────────────────────────┐
│  Title text (bold)     │  ← 40–48 px, white or brand gold
│  Subtitle (light)      │  ← 24–28 px, white 80% opacity
│                        │
│   [Device frame]       │
│                        │
└────────────────────────┘
```

Background: dark teal gradient (`#0B1A2E` → `#1A3A5C`) matches the app's night theme and works well on both stores.
